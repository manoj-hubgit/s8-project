BRINJAL

PH:

  • 6.5-7.5

SEED RATE:

  • Varieties : 1.5 kg/ha.

SEED TREATMENT:

  • Treat seeds with Trichoderma viride 4 g/kg or Pseudomonas fluorescens 10 g/kg or Carbendazim 2 g/kg of seeds before sowing.
  • Treat the seeds with (Carboxyn 37.5% + Thiram 37.5%) DS @ 1.5g or (Carbendazim 1.0g + Thiram 1.5g)/ kg of seeds.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • NURSERY BED: Seeds should be sown in 10cm gap.

  • SOIL: Transplant the seedlings after 30-35 days in soil.

  • Form ridges and furrows at 60cm spacing.

  • Add 25ton/hectare of FYM in soil during ploughing.

  • Apply 2 kg / ha of Azospirillum and 2 kg / ha of Phosphobacteria by mixing with 50 kg of FYM

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply Pendimethalin 1.0 kg a.i. / ha or Fluchloralin 1.0 kg a.i / ha as pre-emergence herbicide, followed by hand weeding once at 30 days after planting

IRRIGATION:

  • After establishment of seedlings, irrigate at weekly intervals.

  • Install the drip irrigation with main and sub main pipes and place lateral tubes at an interval of 1.5 m.

  • Before planting, wet the beds using drip system for 8-12 hrs.

  • Planting to be done at a spacing of 90x60x75 cm in the paired row system, using ropes marked at 75 cm spacing.

  • Spray Pendimethalin 1.0 kg a.i./ha or Fluchloralin 1.0 kg a.i/ha as pre-emergence herbicide at 3rd day after planting.

  • Gap filling can be done at 7th day after transplanting.

HARVEST:

  • Harvest can be done 55 - 60 days after transplanting.

  • Fruits are harvested at tender stage at 4 - 5 days intervals.
