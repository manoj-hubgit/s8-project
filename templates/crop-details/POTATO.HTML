POTATO

PH:

  • 4.8-5.4

SEED RATE:

  • 3000 – 3500 kg/ha

SEED TREATMENT:

  • Use Carbon disulphide 30 g/100 kg of seeds for breaking the dormancy and inducing sprouting of tubers.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • Prepare the land to fine tilth.

  • In hills provide an inward slope in the terraces.

  • Provide drainage channel along the inner edge of the terrace.

  • Form ridges and furrows with a spacing of 45 cm between ridges either by hand hoe or ridger.

  • Use disease free, well spouted tubers weighing 40 – 50 grams for planting. Plant the tubers at 20 cm apart.

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply 15 t/ha of FYM and 2 kg each of Azospirillum and Phosphobacterium as basal and 120 kg N, 240 kg P and 120 kg K/ha in two splits; half as basal and the balance for top dressing on 30 days after sowing. Apply Magnesium sulphate at 60 kg/ha as basal dose.

  • The critical period of weed-competition is upto 60 days and it is essential to keep the field weed-free during that period. Take up the first hoeing on 45th day without disturbing stolons. Second hoeing and earthing can be taken up at 60th day.

IRRIGATION:

  • Irrigate the field copiously immediately after sowing and afterwards as and when necessary.

  • Install drip system with main and sub-main pipes and place the inline lateral tubes at an interval of 1.5 m.

  • Form the raised beds at 120 cm width at an interval of 30cm and place the laterals at the centre of each bed.

  • Fertigation requirement: 120:160:100kg / ha

HARVEST:

  • Average yield is 15 – 20 t/ha of tubers
