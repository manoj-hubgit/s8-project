RIBBED GOURD

PH:

  • 6.5-7.5

SEED RATE:

  • Varieties: 2 kg / ha.

  • Hybrids: 1 Kg / ha.

SEED TREATMENT:

  • The seeds are treated with Trichoderma viride 4 g or Pseudomonas fluorescens 10 g / kg of seeds before sowing.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • Plough the field to fine tilth. Dig pits of 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm size at 2.5 x 2 m spacing and form basins.

  • Sow the seeds (3 seeds/pit) treated with Trichoderma viride @ 4 g or Pseudomonas fluorescens @ 10g/kg of seeds and thin the seedlings to two per pit after 15 days..

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply 10 kg of FYM, 100 g of NPK 6:12:12 mixture as basal dose per pit and N @ 10 g pit 30 days after sowing. Apply Azospirillum and Phosphobacteria @ 2 kg/ha and Pseudomonas 2.5 kg/ha along with FYM 50 kg and neem cake @ 100 kg before last ploughing.

IRRIGATION:

  • Irrigate the basin before dibbling the seeds and thereafter once a week

  • Install drip system with main and sub-main pipes and place the inline lateral tubes at an interval of 1.5 m.

  • Place the drippers in lateral tubes at an interval of 60 cm and 50 cm spacing with 4 LPH and 3.5 LPH capacities respectively

  • Fertigation:

  • Apply a dose of 250:100:100 kg NPK/ha throughout the cropping period through split application.

HARVEST:

  • Hoeing is done thrice to control weed population. Provide support for the plants to reach the pandal erected at a height of 2 m. Spray Ethrel 250 ppm (2.5 ml/10 lit of water) four times commencing from 15th day after sowing at weekly intervals to increase yield.

  • Average yield of Hybrid is 35-40 t/ha in 135 – 175 days.

  • Average yield is 14 – 15 t/ha.
