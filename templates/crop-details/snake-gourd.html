SNAKE GOURD

PH:

  • 6.5-7.5

SEED RATE:

  • Varieties: 1.0 kg / ha.

  • Hybrids: 200 - 250 g / ha.

SEED TREATMENT:

  • Treat the seeds with Trichoderma viride @ 4 g / kg or Pseudomonas fluorescens @ 10 g/ kg and sow in lines spaced at 10 cm in raised nursery beds and cover with sand.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • Plough the field to fine tilth and dig pits of size 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm at 2.5 x 2 m spacing and form basins.

  • Sow the seeds (5 seeds / pit) and thin the seedlings to two/pit after 15 days of sowing

  • Hoeing and weeding can be done thrice or as and when necessary.

  • Provide stakes and train the plants to reach the pandal height (2 m).

  • Pandal is not essential for Co 2 variety.

  • Spray Ethrel 100 ppm (1 ml in 10 lit of water) four times from 10 to 15 days after sowing at weekly intervals.

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply Fluchloralin 1.0 kg a.i / ha as pre-emergence herbicide, followed by hand weeding once at 30 days after planting.

IRRIGATION:

  • After establishment of seedlings, irrigate at weekly intervals.

  • Form raised beds of 120 cm width at an interval of 30 cm. place the laterals at the centre of the each bed.

  • Before planting wet the beds using drip system for 8-12 hrs.

HARVEST:

  • Spray Triacontanol @ 1.25 ml/l on 20, 40, 60 and 80th day of planting. Spray NAA 10 ppm (10 mg/l of water) on 60 and 90 days after planting to increase fruit set.

  • Harvesting can be done 75 days after transplanting. First two picking yield green chilli and subsequently yield red ripe fruits.
