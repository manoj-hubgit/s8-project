CHILLY

PH:

  • 6.5-7.5

SEED RATE:

  • Varieties: 1.0 kg / ha.

  • Hybrids: 200 - 250 g / ha.

SEED TREATMENT:

  • Treat the seeds with Trichoderma viride @ 4 g / kg or Pseudomonas fluorescens @ 10 g/ kg and sow in lines spaced at 10 cm in raised nursery beds and cover with sand.
  • Watering with rose can has to be done
  • daily. Drench the nursery with Copper oxychloride @ 2.5 g/l of water at 15 days interval against damping off disease. Apply Carbofuran 3 G at 10 g/sq.m. at sowing.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • Mix sterilized cocopeat @ 300 kg with 5 kg neem cake along with Azospirillum and phosphobacteria each @ 1 kg.
  • Sow the treated seed in protrays @ 1 seed per cell.
  • Cover the seed with cocopeat and keep the trays one above the other and cover with a polythene sheet till germination starts.
  • After 6 days place the protrays with germinated seedlings individually on the raised beds inside the shade net.
  • with rose can everyday upto seed germination.
  • Drench with NPK 19:19:19 @ 0.5% (5g/l) at 18 days after sowing.
  • After ploughing, Thoroughly prepare the field with the addition of FYM @ 25 t/ ha and form ridges and furrows at a spacing of 60 cm.
  • Apply 2 kg/ha of Azospirillum and 2 kg/ha of Phosphobacteria by mixing with 50 kg of FYM.
  • Irrigate the furrows and transplant 25 days old seedlings on the sides of ridges.
  • Life irrigation should be given on 3rd day of planting.

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply Fluchloralin 1.0 kg a.i / ha as pre-emergence herbicide, followed by hand weeding once at 30 days after planting.

IRRIGATION:

  • After establishment of seedlings, irrigate at weekly intervals.

  • Form raised beds of 120 cm width at an interval of 30 cm. place the laterals at the centre of the each bed.

  • Before planting wet the beds using drip system for 8-12 hrs.

HARVEST:

  • Spray Triacontanol @ 1.25 ml/l on 20, 40, 60 and 80th day of planting. Spray NAA 10 ppm (10 mg/l of water) on 60 and 90 days after planting to increase fruit set.

  • Harvesting can be done 75 days after transplanting. First two picking yield green chilli and subsequently yield red ripe fruits.
