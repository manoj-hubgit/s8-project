TOMATO

PH:

  • 6.5-7.5

SEED RATE:

  • Varieties : 300-350 g / ha

  • Hybrids : 100-150 g / ha

SEED TREATMENT:

  • Treat the seeds with Trichoderma viride 4 g or Carbendazim 2 g per kg of seeds 24 h before sowing.

  • Just before sowing, treat the seeds with Azospirillum @ 40 g / 400 g of seeds.

FIELD PREPARATION AND GROWING:

  • Mix sterilized cocopeat @ 300 kg with 5 kg neem cake along with Azospirillum and Phosphobacteria each @ 1 kg.

  • Sow the treated seed in protrays @ one seed per cell.

  • Cover the seed with cocopeat and keep the trays one above the other and cover with a polythene sheet till germination starts.

  • After 6 days place the protrays with germinated seedlings individually on the raised beds inside the shade net.

  • After 18 days of sowing, drench it in NPK (5g/l)

  • After ploughing, Thoroughly prepare the field with the addition of FYM @ 25 t/ ha and form ridges and furrows at a spacing of 60 cm.

  • Apply 2 kg/ha of Azospirillum and 2 kg/ha of Phosphobacteria by mixing with 50 kg of FYM.

  • Irrigate the furrows and transplant 25 days old seedlings on the sides of ridges.

  • Life irrigation should be given on 3rd day of planting.

FERTILIZER:

  • Apply Fluchloralin 1.0 kg a.i / ha as pre-emergence herbicide, followed by hand weeding once at 30 days after planting.

IRRIGATION:

  • After establishment of seedlings, irrigate at weekly intervals.

  • Install the drip irrigation with main and sub main pipes and place lateral tubes at an interval of 1.5 m.

  • Place the drippers in lateral tubes at an interval of 60 cm and 50 cm spacing with 4 LPH and 3.5 LPH capacities respectively.

  • Form raised beds of 120 cm width at an interval of 30 cm. place the laterals at the centre of each bed.

  • Before planting, wet the beds using drip system for 8-12 hrs.

HARVEST:

  • Spray 1.25 ppm (625 ml in 500 litres of water) Triacontanol at 15 days after transplanting and at full bloom stage to increase the yield.

  • N spray at 2ml / litre in three sprays; 30 days, 55 days and 75 days after planting increase the yield.

  • DURATION: 110- 115 days from transplanting (135 - 140 days from sowing)
